Any Questions? Ask Us!

Styler can be installed in your living room, dressing room, laundry room, anywhere you want it! We recommend you ensure there is ample space above the Styler as well as about 5cm around the sides and back for ventilation. Simply plug in your Styler, fill the water container, and start using your new Styler! For more detailed installation instructions, check out the product manual!

Styler can care for a wide range of things, both clothing and non-clothing items! You can care for your bedding, your children's toys, and of course, your favorite clothing pieces. Styler is also suitable for use on many different types of fabrics including silk, leather, cashmere, and wool. This means that you can toss a dry-clean only silk shirt in the same machine as a pair of yoga pants and get the same great results. We don’t recommend putting shoes in the Styler, and always encourage you to check the clothing label and wash guide of items before putting them in the Styler. Check out some more examples of what Styler cares for in our Styler A-Z video!

If your clothes are still damp or wet at the end of a Styler cycle, there are a few easy solutions to try. Check the filter first, if there is lint in it, try cleaning it out before the next use. Next, make sure no clothes are being trapped in the door and hindering it from closing properly. It’s also possible that your door gasket is damaged, leaving a gap in the rubber seal and affecting the performance of the Styler. In this case, call for service.

A standard Styler cycle uses 1500W or 1.5 kw of power. This is about the same amount of energy used to run an electric heater for an hour. 1.5kw is equal to about $.20 USD of electricity. Electricity rates may vary by user and region.
